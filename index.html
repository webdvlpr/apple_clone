Even more to discover

Wallet. Store your boarding pass, movie tickets or rewards cards for easy access.

Find Devices. Find People. Find Items. Locate devices or get pinged when you leave your iPhone behind. See where your friends are. Track down an AirTag. Do it all from your watch.

Cycle Tracking. Log your period, record symptoms like cramps and get predictions for your next fertile window.8

Camera Remote. Use Apple Watch as a viewfinder for your iPhone camera. See a preview, then set a timer or just snap away.

Siri. Do almost everything on Apple Watch — ask for directions, get a weather report or play a song — just by talking to Siri.

Noise. The Noise app lets you know when high noise levels around you can possibly affect your hearing.

Home. Control lights, a thermostat and the garage door. Or access a security camera to see who is at the front door.

Maps. Get turn-by-turn directions from Maps right from your wrist. You can even receive a tap when it’s time to turn.
